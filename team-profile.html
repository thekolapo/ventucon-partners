Charles Arong
Mentors aren’t people you necessarily like the best at a certain time, mentors are people who pushed you the furthest to help you achieve your goals and targets; Mentors are people who push you to do better.

Mentorship is usually a two-way street, young professionals most times fail to understand that you have to deliver tremendous value to your mentor as well. The process is one of the best paths to career success, the benefits you gain from a good mentor/mentee relationship can outweigh school, natural ability and luck. The key is to have the foresight and humility to ask to be mentored and also stay patient while being mentored.

Marketing is a big area of interest for me. It isn’t the only one, but it is what I have majorly focused on.

There are various stages of business growth, but for us there are five stages for business growth, which are; validation, marketing, creativity, leadership & relevance.

The validation of your business is an important phase of planned business growth, you get your business into the market and validate your business idea with those interested (get to know what people think about your business, industry etc.). Marketing is a stage that helps you get the word out about your business & offers.

Creativity helps you open your mind to new possibilities, innovations that drive company growth to the next level. Leadership shows your team’s drive to achieve its set mission, keeping team commitment levels at an all-time high in order to take any business to the next level of growth. Company relevance is a long term strategy which has to be in mind from the very start of a business (a company that doesn’t think about long-term relevance is a company that can’t last).
