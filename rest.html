about us
The resilient, the innovative, the fighters, the creatives, the underdogs, the philosophers, the underestimated, the quiet titans.

Ventucon partners believe in these type of African founders; these individuals are a rare gem. We partner early-sometimes when it is just a mere idea as we believe that the first decisions have prodigious impact on the success curve.

Our style isn’t for everyone, we push when we see great promise. Our approach will be liked by some and disliked by others, those who believe in success will see where our system leads.

Our Team (Partners, Advisors etc) is a mirror image of the kind of African founders we are willing to work with. Founders who have the will to win and create great societal impact while winning. Our team is filled with winners, overachievers and individuals from humble backgrounds; a team filled with the entrepreneurial spirit who know what it means to walk that path.

We value teamwork, therefore we will be working together with founders and therefore creating Africans entrepreneurs who can stand out for, hard work, humility and innovation. We are a small team with varied strengths, focusing on diverse entrepreneurs who are willing and ready to help each other raise winners.

We are partners not just investors; partners for the long-term. We are ready to partner with audacious Africans around the world build legendary companies.
